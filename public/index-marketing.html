How It Works

Three steps to proven delivery.

01

Orders In

Pharmacy sends delivery orders. LYN's dispatch system assigns routes, optimizes stops, and pushes to drivers.

02

Drivers Deliver

Drivers follow optimized routes with full PRO flow — geofence check, package photo, delivery photo, signature, and delivery note.

03

Proof Delivered

Pharmacy sees proof of delivery instantly. Photos, signatures, GPS, timestamps — all in their portal. No phone calls needed.
